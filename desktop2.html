Overview

The Ferrari Enzo is a famous sports car made by the Italian car company Ferrari. It was named after the founder of the company, Enzo Ferrari. The car was built between 2002 and 2004, and only 400 were made, making it very rare. The Enzo has a powerful V12 engine, which allows it to go extremely fast, reaching top speeds of around 350 km/h (217 mph). It is known for its sleek design, advanced technology, and high performance. The car was built using lightweight materials like carbon fibre, which helped it to be both fast and agile. Today, the Ferrari Enzo is considered a collector's item and is one of the most iconic supercars in the world.
